AI that Listens

The AI Modal offers four ways in: Text, Voice (Quick Command or Record & Transcribe), Document (.docx/.pdf), and Link. Pick your List Type from Bullet, Reminder, Summary, or Brainstorm, and let Star Task do the heavy lifting.

Reminders on iOS can be Banner + Sound or Banner (Silent), repeating on your schedule and updating next-due times seamlessly in the cloud.
