Comprehensive Features of Star Task

Star Task is designed to be your ultimate productivity companion, offering a wide range of features to help you manage your tasks, projects, and time effectively. Below, you'll find a detailed overview of all the features Star Task has to offer.

Easy Task Management

Star Task provides a user-friendly interface to help you organize, prioritize, and manage your tasks efficiently. Here are some key features:

  • Task Creation: Quickly create tasks with just a few taps. Add details such as due dates, priority levels, and notes to ensure everything is covered.
  • Task Organization: Group tasks into lists for better organization. Drag and drop tasks to reorder them based on priority or due date.
  • Subtasks: Break down larger tasks into smaller, manageable subtasks to make progress more achievable.
  • Task Completion: Mark tasks as complete with a simple tap. Completed tasks are moved to a separate section for easy reference.

Beautiful Themes

Star Task offers a variety of stunning themes to personalize your task management experience. Choose from multiple themes to match your style and mood:

  • Daybreak: A bright and cheerful theme available for free users.
  • Alive: A vibrant and energetic theme available to subscribers.
  • Charge: A dynamic theme that keeps you motivated and focused.
  • Elemental Green: A soothing green theme that brings a sense of calm and balance.
  • Peach: A soft and warm theme that adds a touch of elegance to your tasks.
  • Rejuvenating Whisper: A refreshing theme that helps you stay relaxed and productive.
  • Withering Whisper: A mysterious theme that adds depth and intrigue to your task management.
  • Cosmic Dawn: Secure premium theme Cosmic Dawn for free by subscribing during the Star Task launch event. Don't miss out on this limited-time offer!

Reminders and Notifications

Stay on top of your schedule with Star Task's powerful reminders and notifications:

  • Due Date Reminders: Set due dates for your tasks and receive reminders as the deadline approaches.
  • Customizable Notifications: Customize notifications to remind you of important tasks at the right time.
  • Alarm Integration: Use alarms to ensure you never miss a crucial task or deadline.
  • Advanced Reminder Options: Choose from various reminder options, including reminding you when the task is due or in advance.

Attachment Support

Add relevant files and documents to your tasks to keep all your information in one place:

  • File Attachments: Attach files such as documents, images, and PDFs to your tasks for easy reference.
  • Thumbnail Previews: View thumbnail previews of attached files for quick identification.
  • Cloud Storage Integration: Store attachments in the cloud and access them from any device.

Live List Sharing

Collaborate with others in real-time using Star Task's live list sharing feature:

  • Share Lists: Share your task lists with others by entering their email addresses.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Collaborate on tasks in real-time, with changes instantly visible to all members.
  • Permission Management: Manage permissions to control who can view or edit your shared lists.
  • Invitation Management: Send and manage invitations for shared lists, track pending invites, and accept or reject incoming invites.

Task Duplication

Quickly create recurring tasks with Star Task's task duplication feature:

  • Duplicate Tasks: Create duplicates of tasks with a single tap, setting a new due date based on the duplication frequency.
  • Duplication Frequency: Choose from various duplication frequencies, including daily, weekly, and monthly.
  • Efficient Task Management: Extend the lifespan of your task lists without manually recreating each task.

Cloud Backup and Sync

Keep your tasks safe and accessible across all your devices with cloud backup and sync:

  • Automatic Backup: Automatically back up your tasks to the cloud to prevent data loss.
  • Sync Across Devices: Access your tasks from any device, ensuring you're always up-to-date.
  • Restore Tasks: Easily restore tasks from the cloud if you switch devices or reinstall the app.

Subscription Options

Unlock premium features with a Star Task subscription:

  • Monthly Subscription: Access premium themes, advanced features, and increased task limits for $4.99 per month.
  • Yearly Subscription: Enjoy the same premium benefits with a yearly subscription for $49.99.
  • Free Tier: Access basic features, including 3 lists and 3 tasks per list, with the Daybreak theme.

Account and Content Management

Manage your account and content with ease:

  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data with a single tap.
  • Content Deletion: Choose to delete only your content while keeping your account active.
  • Privacy and Security: Sign in securely using Google OAuth to protect your data.

Advanced Task Features

Take advantage of advanced features to enhance your productivity:

  • Priority Levels: Assign priority levels to tasks to ensure the most important items are addressed first.
  • Tags: Use tags to categorize and filter tasks for better organization.
  • Highlight Tasks: Highlight important tasks to make them stand out.
  • Sort and Filter: Sort tasks by name or due date and filter them based on completion status.
  • Task Search: Use the search feature to quickly find specific tasks within your lists.

Upcoming Due Dates

Keep track of your upcoming tasks with the Upcoming Due Dates screen:

  • Quick Overview: View all your tasks with approaching due dates in one place.
  • Filter by List: Filter upcoming tasks by list for more focused planning.
  • Stay Organized: Ensure you never miss an important deadline by keeping an eye on your upcoming tasks.

Save and Load Lists

Save and load your task lists for better data management:

  • Save Lists: Save your task lists to the cloud for future reference.
  • Load Lists: Load saved lists from the cloud to restore your tasks.
  • Backup Management: Manage your saved lists and backups directly from the app.

User Profiles

Manage your user profile within Star Task:

  • Profile Picture: Display your Google profile picture within the app.
  • Account Information: View and manage your account details, including email and subscription status.

Enhanced Collaboration

Collaborate seamlessly with others using Star Task's advanced collaboration features:

  • Manage Shared Lists: Manage lists shared with you and lists you've shared with others.
  • Track Invitations: Track invitations you send out and receive from others, managing them efficiently.
  • Real-Time Updates: Enjoy real-time updates on shared lists to ensure everyone stays informed.

Accessibility

Star Task is designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring everyone can use the app effectively:

  • Accessible Design: Features an intuitive and accessible design for users of all abilities.
  • Customizable Themes: Choose from light, dark, or system themes to match your preferences and needs.

Conclusion

Star Task is packed with features designed to make your task management experience as efficient and enjoyable as possible. Explore these features and discover how Star Task can help you stay organized, productive, and on top of your tasks. Start using Star Task today and take your productivity to the next level!
