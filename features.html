Comprehensive Features of Star Task

Star Task is designed to be your ultimate productivity companion, offering a wide range of features to help you manage your tasks, projects, and time effectively. Below, you'll find a detailed overview of all the features Star Task has to offer.

Easy Task Management

Star Task provides a user-friendly interface to help you organize, prioritize, and manage your tasks efficiently. Here are some key features:

  • Task Creation: Quickly create tasks with just a few taps. Add details such as due dates, priority levels, and notes to ensure everything is covered.
  • Task Organization: Group tasks into lists for better organization. Drag and drop tasks to reorder them based on priority or due date.
  • Expand/Collapse Tasks: Use the expand/collapse button to easily manage long tasks, allowing you to shrink or expand tasks as needed to focus on what's important.
  • Task Completion: Mark tasks as complete with a simple tap. Completed tasks are moved to a separate section for easy reference.
  • Thumbnail Previews: View thumbnail previews of tasks for quick identification.

Beautiful Themes

Star Task offers a variety of stunning themes to personalize your task management experience. Each theme supports both light and dark modes, letting you customize the look and feel of the app to match your style and mood:

  • Daybreak: A bright and cheerful theme designed to inspire positivity and energy. Available for free users.
  • Alive: A vibrant and dynamic theme that energizes your workflow. Available to subscribers.
  • Charge: A bold and motivating theme that keeps you focused on your goals. Available to subscribers.
  • Elemental Green: A soothing green theme that fosters calm and balance during busy days. Available to subscribers.
  • Peach: A warm and elegant theme that adds a touch of softness to your tasks. Available to subscribers.
  • Rejuvenating Whisper: A refreshing and tranquil theme perfect for staying relaxed and productive. Available to subscribers.
  • Withering Whisper: A mysterious and captivating theme that brings depth and intrigue to task management. Available to subscribers.
  • Cosmic Dawn: A radiant and cosmic-themed experience, available for free to all users who download Star Task or purchase a subscription. Celebrate productivity in style!

AI-Powered Features

Star Task leverages cutting-edge AI technology to transform your productivity experience. Our AI features are more advanced than ever:

  • AI Quick List: Effortlessly create task lists by entering a prompt. The AI generates context-aware tasks with optional reminders, tailored to your needs.
  • AI Note Taking: Capture thoughts or meeting notes through voice-to-text technology. Star Task intelligently organizes them into actionable task lists, complete with reminders where applicable.
  • Custom API Key Support: Use your own OpenAI API key for a personalized experience. Gain control over your AI interactions and optimize for performance.
  • AI Model Selection: Choose between GPT-3.5 and GPT-4.0 for tailored results. Enjoy flexibility and precision based on your needs and preferences.
  • Reminder Type Options: Customize AI-powered reminders with choices like:
    • Auto: Let Star Task decide the best reminder type for you.
    • Alarm: Set alarms to ensure critical tasks aren't missed.
    • Notification: Opt for subtle notifications for non-urgent tasks.
    • Both: Combine alarms and notifications for comprehensive task management.
  • AI-Powered Reminders: Currently available for AI Quick List and AI Note Taking. These reminders integrate seamlessly into your workflow, ensuring you stay on top of your tasks and ideas.

Reminders and Notifications

Stay organized with Star Task's advanced reminders and notifications:

  • AI-Powered Reminder Customization: Choose how AI-powered reminders behave for your tasks using Auto, Alarm, Notification, or Both.
  • Due Date Reminders: Receive timely alerts as deadlines approach.
  • Exact Alarm Integration: Enjoy precise alarms that function even in Do Not Disturb or silent mode. Customize snooze options and alarm sounds to fit your style.
  • Daily Repeat Alarms: Set daily alarms for recurring tasks like medication or workout reminders.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Use advanced options to schedule reminders in advance, repeat on custom schedules, or set reminders exactly when tasks are due.

AI Note Taking

Turn your thoughts into actionable lists with AI-powered note-taking:

  • Voice-to-Text Technology: Speak naturally, and Star Task transcribes your words in real-time.
  • Smart Organization: Automatically structures notes into task lists, complete with deadlines and optional reminders.
  • Context-Aware Suggestions: Identifies key points in your notes and suggests reminders, subtasks, or deadlines.
  • Seamless Integration: Add AI-generated lists directly into your task manager for easy access across all devices.
  • Enhanced Reminders: AI Note Taking includes optional AI-powered reminders for your transcriptions.

Whether you're brainstorming, planning, or capturing meeting notes, Star Task's AI Note Taking elevates your productivity.

Subtasks

Break down larger tasks into smaller, manageable subtasks to make progress more achievable:

  • Task Breakdown: Create subtasks to break down complex tasks into more manageable steps.
  • Clickable Links: Add clickable links to your subtasks, making it easy to access important references, resources, or web pages directly from your task list.
  • Progress Tracking: Track your progress as you complete each subtask and move closer to achieving your main task.

Live List Sharing

Collaborate with others in real-time using Star Task's live list sharing feature:

  • Share Lists: Share your task lists with others by entering their email addresses.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Collaborate on tasks in real-time, with changes instantly visible to all members.
  • Permission Management: Manage permissions to control who can view or edit your shared lists.
  • Invitation Management: Send and manage invitations for shared lists, track pending invites, and accept or reject incoming invites.

Task Duplication

Quickly create recurring tasks with Star Task's task duplication feature:

  • Duplicate Tasks: Create duplicates of tasks with a single tap, setting a new due date based on the duplication frequency.
  • Duplication Frequency: Choose from various duplication frequencies, including daily, weekly, and monthly.
  • Efficient Task Management: Extend the lifespan of your task lists without manually recreating each task.

Cloud Backup and Sync

Never lose track of your tasks with Star Task’s reliable cloud backup and sync. Stay organized and stress-free, knowing your tasks are always safe and accessible:

  • Automatic Backup: Your tasks are automatically saved to the cloud, so you never have to worry about losing your data.
  • Sync Across Devices: Seamlessly access your tasks from any device, ensuring you’re always up-to-date, whether on your phone, tablet, or elsewhere.
  • Restore Tasks: Switching devices or reinstalling the app? Effortlessly restore all your tasks and pick up right where you left off.

Advanced Task Features

Supercharge your productivity with Star Task's advanced task management tools:

  • Priority Levels: Focus on what matters most by assigning priority levels to your tasks, from low to urgent.
  • Labels: Categorize and organize tasks more effectively with labels, making it easy to find what you need at a glance.
  • Highlight Tasks: Make critical tasks stand out with a single tap, so they’re always top of mind.

Enhanced Collaboration

Collaborate seamlessly with others using Star Task's advanced collaboration features:

Accessibility

Star Task is built with accessibility at its core, ensuring everyone can manage tasks effectively and comfortably:

  • Accessible Design: Enjoy a clean and intuitive interface designed to accommodate users of all abilities, including those with visual, cognitive, or motor challenges.
  • Customizable Text Size: Adjust text sizes for list names, task details, and notes to suit your reading preferences and needs.
  • Customizable Themes: Choose from light, dark, or system themes, or add a personalized background image with subtle opacity for better readability and style.
